  • What to do in Zion National Park with Kids
  • Canyon Overlook
    • Length: 1.0 mi, Est. 29m
    • Elevation gain: 187 ft, out & back
    • along dramatic canyon walls and to an overlook perched over Zion Canyon
    • eastside of the park, will drive through Zion-Mt. Carmel Tunnel
  • Need to take shuttle for the northside of the park
    • first bus into: 8am
    • last bus out: 5:45pm
  • Riverside Walk
    • Take shuttle to #9
    • Length: 1.9 mi, Est. 44m
    • Elevation gain: 193 ft, out & back
    • right along the Virgin River and into a deep canyon
    • end of this trail is the start of The Narrows
  • The Grotto Trail
    • Take shuttle to #6, walk to #5 (Zion Lodge)
    • Length: 1.2 mi, Est. 25m
    • Elevation gain: 78 ft, out & back
  • Map for the day
  • Need to take shuttle for the northside of the park
    • first bus into: 8am
    • last bus out: 5:45pm

  • Pipe Spring NM
    • open daily from 8:30am to 4:30pm
    • 70-min dr, set off @ 7am
    • 25-minute introductory video
    • learn about:
      • the history of the Kaibab Paiutes
      • the movement of Mormon settlers into the area
    • Winsor Castle ranger guided tours
  • Vermilion Cliffs Scenic Highway
    • Vermillion Cliffs Scenic Highway Road Trip
    • Le Fevre Overlook
      • the first major scenic pullout along the Vermillion Cliffs Scenic Highway heading eastbound
      • a great place from which to view all layers in the Grand Staircase geological formation
      • can see all the way to Bryce National Park and Grand-Staircase Escalante National Monument from Le Fevre Overlook on a clear day
    • Pasture Canyon Lookout
      • terrific views of the plateau stretching for miles from an elevated position
    • Marble Canyon
      • marks the beginning of the Grand Canyon in northern Arizona
    • Navajo Bridge
      • best views of Marble Canyon are from the historic Navajo Bridge
      • can walk across the original bridge
  • Horseshoe Bend
    • 1.5-mile roundtrip hike is required to reach the overlook platform
    • car parking fee: $10
    • sunset around 5pm
  • Carl Hayden Visitor Center
    • Main visitor center for Glen Canyon National Recreation Area and Rainbow Bridge National Monument
    • hours: 9am-4pm
